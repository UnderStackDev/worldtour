üåü The Philosophy: Experience > Competition
Most apps want you to compete, grind, and stress about streaks. World Tour is different.

No Leaderboards: This is your personal travel diary.

Gastro Loot: Watching a movie from Spain? Eat some tapas. Drinking a warm beer counts too. It's about the vibe, not the performance.

Smart AI Curator: Don't know what to watch from Bhutan? The built-in smart prompt helps you ask your own AI for tailored recommendations based on your progress and ratings.



üì¶ Features

Interactive Map: A massive, zoomable world map that tracks your progress visually.

Two Editions:

Movie Edition: For the deep dive (2h commitment).

Music Edition: For the daily explorer (3min commitment). Includes "Rare Loot" for listening to full albums.

Privacy Focused: Runs 100% in your browser's Local Storage. Your data stays on your device.
